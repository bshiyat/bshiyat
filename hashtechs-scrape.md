## About

HashTechs is a leading regional provider of business solutions serving the global market, known for delivering beautiful apps and websites. The company has invested in creating multiple startups and subsidiaries, and through its partners and sister companies it supports more than **600+ business users**.

With **7+ years of experience** in business applications on the MENA level, HashTechs employs over **23 professional full-time team members**. The company's philosophy centres on investing in human capital — treating employees as its main assets and building a work environment that promotes continuous growth and learning.

The company aspires to become the **first choice for IT development in the MENA region**.
